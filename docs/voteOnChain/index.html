Use case description

The use case concerns the management of secure voting.

How we built it

The system uses the Dizme platform for voter identity management and the blockchain Algorand for voting. In addition to the voters, the actors involved are the Organization that calls an election and therefore identifies the voters and gives them the right to vote. A Trusted Third Party (TTP), which manages the voting by verifying who has the right to vote and providing them with the ballot paper. Both the Organization and the TTP use Dizme to verify identity credentials and right to vote. The vote is expressed through an Algorand transaction carried out directly by the voter.
